# CryoCore Figure Dossier

Use this skill for ChimeraX, Mol*, MolViewSpec, Blender, PyMOL, 3Dmol.js, or
other figure and web-viewer outputs.

## Read First

- `campaigns/publishable-figure-dossier/README.md`
- `references/validation-gates.md`
- `docs/chimerax-shared-posture.md`
- `docs/tooling-and-licensing.md`

## Rules

- Figures must be nonblank, labeled, reproducible, and joined to source
  accessions and hashes.
- Captions must include contour levels, software versions, renderer posture, and
  caveats.
- ChimeraX and Incentive PyMOL are runtime-gated; Mol*, MolViewSpec,
  open-source PyMOL, Blender, and 3Dmol.js can be public-safe when terms and
  source compliance are handled.
- Keep scripts, sessions, viewer state, and command ledgers with the dossier.
