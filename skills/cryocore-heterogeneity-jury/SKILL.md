# CryoCore Heterogeneity Jury

Use this skill when work involves continuous heterogeneity, discrete states,
latent spaces, particle subsets, flexible fitting, or ensemble claims.

## Tool Posture

- cryoDRGN and DynaMight are planned open lanes with source-compliance tracking.
- RECOVAR is use-context gated until exact source/package/license posture is
  recorded per campaign.
- CryoSPARC 3DVA/3DFlex is runtime/license gated.
- SOLVAR, CryoHype, CryoDyna, CryoFM-derived methods, and similar preprints are
  watch unless a separate audit promotes them.

## Evidence Rules

- Record input particle/map references and preprocessing provenance.
- Record latent coordinates, state-selection rationale, particle subsets, and
  negative or ambiguous states.
- Emit a disagreement matrix when multiple methods are compared.
- Do not make biological mechanism claims without map/model validation and
  explicit caveats.
