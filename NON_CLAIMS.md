# Non-Claims

CryoCore can help assemble cryo-EM evidence dossiers, validation scaffolds, provider contracts, and claim ledgers. It does not by itself prove biological mechanism, ligand action, therapeutic value, safety, manufacturability, clinical relevance, or regulatory acceptability.

## What The Repo Can Support

- public-accession metadata ledgers
- raw-to-map and map/model workflow contracts
- stage-progress and provider closeout checks
- map/model fit, figure, and claim-ledger schemas
- public-safe demos and tiny fixtures
- license posture and operator-gate scaffolding

## What Requires External Evidence

- final density interpretation
- atomic model correctness
- conformational-state assignment
- ligand mechanism or binding claims
- publication-grade biological conclusions without expert review and independent evidence
- clinical or therapeutic claims

All such claims require real artifacts, expert review, and the appropriate experimental, institutional, and legal context.
